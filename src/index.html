About Us

Team Devil's Werk

We started in the humble area of Los Angeles, California in 2008 with a specialty in three-piece wheels.

With 15 years of experience in modifying, repairing, and refinishing all sizes and types of wheels, we provide the highest quality wheels and services that customers trust and love.

We have a deep inventory of wheels in addition to source or customize any wheel to your liking. Having worked with customers across many cities and multiple states, we promise to deliver to your expectations!
